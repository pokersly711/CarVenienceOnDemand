Popular Questions

How often should I have my vehicle washed?

It is recommended by top experts such as BMW UK (https://discover.bmw.co.uk/help/ownership/how-often-clean-my-car), to keep damaging external enfluences that can damage your car from depreciating the value, a car should be cleaned once every 1-2 weeks.

When should I wash off insect residue?

J.D.Power suggests cleaning bug residue immediately, and adding a protectant to the front of your car to make future residue easier to clean off. (https://nadaguides.com/Cars/Shopping-Guides/how-to-remove-bugs-from-a-car). We use Chemical Guys bug plus tar remover when needed in our foam guns to clean and protect your paint.

What kind of car wash should I choose?

We use Chemical Guys HydroSuds High-gloss Hyper Foaming Si02 Ceremic Car Wash Soap and their signature Foam gun for a paint safe touchless Wash.

Is a touchless car wash the safest?

Chemical Guys foam gun and shampoo products leave your paint job good and clean, and slick to the touch when hand dried for fresh wax and protectant.
